Project 1: Yelp Review Text Analytics

Business Question: What issues in customer reviews are most strongly associated with low ratings?

Methods: I cleaned 100,000 Yelp reviews, converted the text into TF-IDF features, and used KMeans clustering to group similar reviews into major themes. I then compared average rating and low-rating share across themes.

Key Finding: Service Problems and Delays was the most damaging review theme. It had the lowest average rating (2.56) and the highest low-rating share (0.593).

Business Value: This analysis helps managers identify the issues that hurt customer satisfaction most, so they can prioritize operational improvements.
